BT Audio Firmware Update

for shitty SparkFun board

Click the button below to launch the official Espressif web flasher:

Open ESP Launchpad

Instructions

  1. Connect your BT Audio device to your computer via USB.
  2. Click Open ESP Launchpad above.
  3. Select the serial port (e.g., “USB‑SERIAL CH340”).
  4. Choose the firmware version shown in Launchpad.
  5. Click Flash and wait for completion.
  6. Your device will restart automatically.

Note: If your device doesn’t auto‑enter flash mode, press and hold the BOOT button while connecting, or when prompted during flashing.
