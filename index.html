Instructions

  1. Connect your BT Audio device to your computer via USB
  2. Click "Install Firmware" above
  3. Select the correct serial port (usually "USB-SERIAL CH340")
  4. Wait for the installation to complete
  5. Your device will restart automatically

Note: You may need to hold the BOOT button on your device when connecting, or when prompted during installation.
